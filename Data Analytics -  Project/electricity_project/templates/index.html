Conclusion

Electricity Consumption Insights

  • ✔ Maharashtra recorded the highest electricity consumption in 2019 and 2020.
  • ✔ Gujarat and Tamil Nadu remain among the top energy consuming states.
  • ✔ 2020 shows a slight overall decline compared to 2019.
  • ✔ Industrialized states demonstrate significantly higher electricity demand.
  • ✔ Sikkim and Mizoram record the lowest electricity consumption levels.

Quarterwise Analysis

Quarterwise Electricity Insights

  • ✔ Q3 2019 recorded the highest electricity consumption (~326K units).
  • ✔ Electricity demand remained consistently high throughout 2019.
  • ✔ A sharp decline is observed across all quarters in 2020.
  • ✔ Q3 2020 shows the lowest recorded consumption (~52K units).
  • ✔ The drop indicates reduced industrial and commercial activity.

Regional Analysis

Regional Electricity Insights

  • ✔ Western Region recorded the highest consumption in both years.
  • ✔ Northern and Southern regions were major contributors in 2019.
  • ✔ 2020 shows a significant decline across all regions.
  • ✔ Eastern Region maintains comparatively lower usage levels.
  • ✔ Regional ranking pattern remains consistent year-on-year.
